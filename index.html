The Royal Compass: Modulo

I. The Positive Path: Modulo reveals the remainder. If you divide 17 by 5, the remainder is 2. Mathematically, $17 \equiv 2 \pmod 5$.

II. The Negative Path: For negative numbers like $-3 \pmod{10}$, simply add the modulus until positive: $-3 + 10 = 7$.
